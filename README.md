# Research Project:An exploration into the learning effectiveness and interest of junior high school students by integrating role-playing games into organic chemistry teaching

This research project was completed as a mandatory requirement for students enrolled in Chingshin Academy's Programming Specialization, which requires a competitive entrance examination for admission. As a team in the Mechatronics Integration Group, we investigated whether game-based learning could serve as a more effective alternative to traditional handout-based instruction in junior high school chemistry education. The paper was later submitted to the National Senior High School Short Essay Competition organized by the Ministry of Education, where it received an "Outstanding" award in the 12th grade category, which is the highest level of recognition in the competition.
